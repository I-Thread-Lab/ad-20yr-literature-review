# AD World Model · 20-Year Nature/Science Literature Review (2005–2026)

A strategic literature review synthesizing **139 papers** from Nature, Science, Nature Medicine, Cell, Lancet and related top-tier venues on Alzheimer's disease research over the past 20 years.

🌐 **Live page**: https://i-thread-lab.github.io/ad-20yr-literature-review/

## What's inside

- **AD patient world model** — 5-layer causal system (molecular → cellular → physiology → behavior → social) reconstructed from literature
- **20-year milestone timeline** — annotated key events from PiB-PET 2004 to ProtAIDe-Dx 2026, with detailed notes on multi-agent and wearable milestones
- **8 open scientific questions** — what AD research has *not* settled
- **Cure status + monitoring's role** — what current treatments can and cannot do, and where wearable/AI can plug in
- **4 literature tables** (sortable, searchable):
  - Table A · Mechanisms + Genetics (27 papers)
  - Table B · Biomarkers + Diagnostics (38 papers)
  - Table C · Therapeutics + Trials (40 papers)
  - Table D · Risk Factors + Digital Health (34 papers)
- **White-space map** — 4 positions in AD research that remained unoccupied as of mid-2026
- **3 candidate research directions** with objective tradeoff comparison
